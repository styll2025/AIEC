Process

How It Works

01

Registration

Complete the registration form with contact details. Provide evidence of CAIP, CAIE, or CAIA certification and information on expertise, sectors, regions and areas of focus.

02

We Build Your Profile

AIEC reviews your submission, verifies your certification with the issuing body, and designs a professional profile that aligns with the AIEC publication standards.

03

Confirmation & Next Steps

We confirm your listing and share the profile link. Organizations can then review credentials and book consultations directly with the professional.
