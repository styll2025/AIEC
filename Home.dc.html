Standards

Recognised professional certifications

CAIP

CERTIFIED AI PROFESSIONAL

A Certified AI Professional (CAIP) understands the technical foundations and future of AI, from core machine learning to deep learning and neural networks. They can assess the impact of intelligent machines in the workplace, navigate ethical and legal considerations, and identify practical AI opportunities to drive business value.

CAIE

CERTIFIED AI ETHICIST

The Certified AI Ethicist demonstrates trustworthy and safe AI by applying key ethical principles, aligning with regulatory objectives, operationalising practical compliance with emerging laws, and confidently navigating the oxethica governance platform to create AI systems that hold up to scrutiny.

CAIA

CERTIFIED AI AUDITOR

The Certified AI Auditor is able to conduct compliance audits of AI systems, ensuring they meet legal requirements, demonstrate technical robustness, and align with ethical mandates, and complete the documentation required to meet regulatory mandates according to region and to the kind of AI system.

Issued by Oxethica and mapped to Oxford Saïd: CAIP (Oxford AI Programme), CAIE (Oxford AI Ethics, Regulation & Compliance Programme), CAIA (both).
